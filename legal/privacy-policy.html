Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use Pharmron Natriceuticals, we may collect the following information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and shipping address provided during checkout or subscription.
  • Payment Information: Payment details are processed securely by Paystack. We do not store credit card numbers.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, and interactions with our site for analytics purposes.
  • Communications: Messages sent via WhatsApp or our contact forms.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and fulfill your orders.
  • To communicate with you about your orders, inquiries, and support requests.
  • To send occasional updates about new products or promotions (only with your consent).
  • To improve our website and product offerings.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with trusted third parties who assist in operating our website and business, provided they agree to keep your information confidential. This includes:

  • Paystack (payment processing)
  • WhatsApp / Meta (customer communication)
  • Analytics providers (anonymized usage data)

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information. All transactions are encrypted via SSL/TLS. Payment data is handled directly by Paystack, which is PCI-DSS compliant.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time.
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

To exercise these rights, contact us via WhatsApp at +234 803 734 1221.

6. Cookies

We use minimal cookies necessary for site functionality (e.g., cart persistence via localStorage). We do not use tracking cookies for advertising without explicit consent.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

8. Contact

For questions about this policy, please contact us:
